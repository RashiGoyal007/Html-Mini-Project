Taco Trivia

When and how were tacos discovered?

Tacos originated in ancient Mesoamerica, where indigenous people used corn tortillas to hold fillings like fish and beans. The modern term "taco" emerged in 18th-century Mexico, evolving into diverse regional styles and spreading globally in the 20th century.
